About Me

Your Licensed Zumba Instructor

Hi! I'm Rocio, a certified ZIN™ (Zumba Instructor Network) member with over 8 years of experience bringing joy, health, and rhythm to students of all levels. I'm passionate about creating an inclusive and energetic environment where everyone feels welcome.

Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned dancer, my classes are designed to meet you where you are. I offer classes in both English and Spanish to make sure everyone can follow along and have fun!
